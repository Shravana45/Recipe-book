Biryani

Ingredients: Rice, curd, oil, spices, chicken, onion, garlic, tomato

Instructions: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, and green chilies to the pan. Add garam masala, chili powder, carrots, peas and beans. Pour in 8 cups of water. Add the rice. Add the chicken.

Bread Omlette

Ingredients: Slices of bread, Eggs, Ghee, Cheese, salt as needed

Instructions: Break the eggs in a bowl and set aside. Add in the salt and turmeric powder. Heat butter in a pan until hot. Add two bread slices on top of the omelette. Sprinkle black pepper. Be lavish with black pepper. Freshly ground black pepper adds a nice aroma and flavor. Sprinkle a little coriander leaves and green chillies. Serve hot with tea
